Jerk Chicken



Serves 4

Prep Time Cook Time Extra Time Total Time
10 min 15 min 1 hr 1 hr, 25 min

Ingredients

    • 1/2 small onion, finely chopped
    • 2 tablespoons soft dark brown sugar
    • 3 tablespoons soya sauce
    • 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
    • small handful chopped fresh thyme
    • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
    • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
    • 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
    • 1 small red chili, sliced
    • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast fillets - cut into strips

Prep the Sauce

  1. Combine the onion, brown sugar, soy sauce, vinegar, thyme, sesame oil, garlic, allspice and chili in the container of a food processor or blender. Process until smooth.
  2. Place the chicken into a large resealable bag, and pour in 3/4 of the mixture. Save the remaining mixture for serving.
  3. Squeeze out excess air, and seal. Marinate in the refrigerator for at least one hour.

Bake the Chicken

  1. Preheat your oven's broiler without the tray.
  2. Remove chicken from the bag, and discard marinade.
  3. Place chicken on tray and broil for 10 to 15 minutes, turning once to ensure even cooking.

Serve

Heat reserved 1/4 marinade in a small pan to make a sauce and pour over chicken when serving.
