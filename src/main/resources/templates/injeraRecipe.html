Injera




Serves 6

Prep Time Cook Time Extra Time Total Time
10 min 10 min 48 hr 2 day, 20 min

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup teff flour (found in health food shops)
  • 3½ cups water
  • pinch of salt

Directions

  1. Mix teff with the water. Leave to stand in a covered bowl for a couple of days until it starts to bubble. (Standing time 2 days.)
  2. The mixture should be slightly sour and the consistency of crêpe batter. Stir in the salt.
  3. Add a ladle of the mixture to a pre-heated skillet and cook slowly until air bubbles rise to the top.
  4. Do not let it brown, and make sure that it is not too thick.
